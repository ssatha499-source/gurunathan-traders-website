Our Story

Built on Local Trust

Gurunathan Traders started with one simple mission: make essential hardware and building materials easy to access for nearby villages around Hosur.

Over the years, plumbers, electricians, painters and contractors have trusted us for consistent stock, fair pricing, and quick guidance on material choices.

Whether it is a small home repair or a full construction site order, our team helps customers pick the right products without confusion.

Who we serve

  • Plumbers and service technicians
  • Electricians and maintenance teams
  • Painters and finish contractors
  • Village households and local builders
